Tuition

All of our open classes run at the price of just $55 per month! This guarantees your dancer 4 classes within the month (once each week). In the case of an absence, dancers are allowed to attend a “make-up”class to ensure that you get you 4 classes in each month. Make-up classes can be scheduled with the front desk via email. Multi-Class & Sibling discounts of 10% are offered.

Register online!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sit and watch my dancer take class?

Absolutely! We provide stadium seating and benches in our studio to allow for parents to watch their dancers. We believe that if the parent is willing to invest in their dancer, they should be able to watch them grow as well!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay a registration fee?

Dancers that register on the day of their FREE trial class will have the registration fee of $35 WAIVED.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my dancer get to perform in a recital?

Yes! Dancers will begin learning their recital routine about 2.5 months before the recital date. This allows them to have plenty of practice before their big day. The fee for this event is separate from tuition which includes their costume, participation fee, custom trophy, and a video copy of the show. Dance Deluxe hosts two different recitals: Winter Showcase (December) & Summer Recital (June).

Frequently Asked Questions

Can my dancer join in the middle of the month?

Of course! Our system automatically pro-rates the tuition of our dancers upon registration, so you only have to pay for the classes that you will be receiving.
