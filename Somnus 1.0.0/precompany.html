Pre-Company

Our Pre-Company Teams consist of intermediate to advances dancers that range from the ages of 5 - 15. These team members participation the styles of ballet, jazz, tap, musical theatre, & acro. Additionally, these members have an intense focus on improving technical abilities by participating in classes such as stretch, legs, turns, leaps, & strength.

Our Pre-Company members are divided up by age:

  • Petite Pre-Company (5-7) $260
  • Mini Pre-Company (7 - 9) $260
  • Junior Pre-Company (9 - 11) $260
  • Teen Pre-Company (12 - 15) $260

  • CORE Classes
