The benefits of company reach far beyond just learning to dance and winning trophies. Dancers build lifelong friendships with the hours spent training, competing and socializing together. They learn the value of teamwork, to trust in each other and to depend on one another. These important lessons will carry them on any path they choose to take in life.

Our company members intensely train 10 - 14 hours per week with the most dedicated faculty in the state. Our well-rounded curriculum consists of ballet, pointe (invitation only), contemporary, jazz, combo classes, technical classes, lyrical, strength, ballroom, acro, hip hop, & more.
